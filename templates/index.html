The Problem

Millions of tonnes of waste are mismanaged every year because people are unsure how to correctly sort and dispose of everyday items. Incorrect disposal of plastic, e-waste, and organic materials leads to pollution, wasted resources, and harm to ecosystems — directly undermining progress on responsible consumption and production.

Our Solution

WasteWise AI lets users upload a photo of a waste item and instantly receive a simulated AI classification into one of six categories, along with clear recycling instructions, environmental impact facts, and disposal recommendations — empowering everyday people to make eco-friendly choices.

Why Waste Segregation Matters (SDG 12)

UN Sustainable Development Goal 12 calls for responsible consumption and production by reducing waste through prevention, reduction, recycling, and reuse. Sorting waste correctly at the source keeps recyclable materials out of landfills, prevents hazardous items like batteries from contaminating soil and water, and allows valuable resources to be reused instead of extracted again. Even small, everyday actions — like separating plastic, paper, glass, metal, e-waste, and organic waste — add up to a major reduction in pollution and resource consumption.
